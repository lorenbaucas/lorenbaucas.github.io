1. Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Pixeland Pixel Art Editor ("the Application", "we", "our") handles user data, with a specific focus on our integration with third-party cloud storage providers like Google Drive.

Google Drive API Disclosure & Usage

The Application requests access to the Google Drive API to provide cross-device project availability. We adhere strictly to the following parameters:

  • Manual Synchronization Only: Cloud backup and retrieval operations to Google Drive are triggered exclusively and manually by the user. The Application never uploads, modifies, or deletes cloud files without your direct input.
  • Local Auto-Backup Separation: The Application features an automatic local backup system that saves your working canvas progress every two (2) minutes. This automatic backup is performed entirely locally on your physical device storage and does not interact with, transfer to, or use your Google Drive quota.
  • Scope and Limitations: We only access, create, or modify files that are directly generated by Pixeland Pixel Art Editor (such as specialized project project files or exported images). We do not read, view, or scan any other personal files stored within your Google Drive account.

Data Sharing and Retention

We do not own external servers to process or store your art. All pixel art data remains on your local hardware or your private cloud storage. Consequently, your personal information or authentication credentials are never collected, sold, or shared with third-party entities, advertising networks, or data brokers.
